# Survivor Log - Day 26

**Location:** The Forgotten Tunnels (Maintenance Depot)  
**Weather:** The rain drums a steady rhythm against the glass canopy above, turning the fungal groves into a shimmering, bioluminescent sea. The air smells like wet concrete and something... older.  

---

Last night's scavenging run through the maintenance tunnels was eerily quiet. The rain masked my footsteps perfectly, but it also made the fungal growths pulse with unnatural light. I found:  
- A **serrated knife** wedged in a maintenance panel (still sticky with something I don't want to think about)  
- Three cans of **food rations** in a locker marked "QUARANTINE USE ONLY"  
- A **fungal Geiger counter** that clicks whenever I point it toward the collapsed tunnel  

The Watchers left another warning scrawled in charcoal near the generator room: *"THEY REMEMBER THROUGH THE ROOTS"*. I don't know if they mean the Fog Dwellers or something worse.  

Dr. Voss emerged from the fungal groves at dawn, his arm veins glowing faintly blue. He claims the knife I found is a "harvesting tool" used by the Fog Dwellers to collect "communicative fruiting bodies." When I asked what that meant, he just pressed a trembling finger to the collapsed tunnel and whispered: *"Listen to the walls after midnight."*  

The rain has turned the western flooded passage into a swirling current. I can hear metal groaning down there - maybe another train car shifting? My fungal sensitivity makes the spores cling to my clothes like static, but at least I can sense the densest clusters before walking into them.  

Morale is low. The hive whispers sound like children laughing in the distance today.  

---  

**Plans for Today:**  
1. Test the Geiger counter near the ventilation shaft  
2. Boil rainwater in the pressure cooker  
3. Reinforce the depot doors with scrap metal before nightfall  

God, I miss sunlight.
