# Survivor Log - Day 29

**Location:** Maintenance Depot  
**Weather:** Howling storm rattling the tunnel grates  

The fungal Geiger counter hasn't stopped clicking since the storm began. It's like the entire tunnel network is *breathing* beneath me, those fungal growths pulsing in time with the thunder. I tried signaling last night - aimed my flashlight through that broken glass canopy where the Watchers left their warnings. Three long, three short. Doubt anyone saw it through the horizontal rain, but the act itself felt... important.

Found something disturbing near the collapsed tunnel this morning. The Geiger counter went wild near a patch of what looked like freshly turned earth. Scraped at it with my knife and uncovered *tunnels* - not ours, not human-sized. Smooth-walled, reeking of that same fungal musk. Whatever's digging these moves fast and knows the subway better than I do. The Watchers' warnings about flooding making hive activity worse suddenly make too much sense.

The storm's turned the western corridors into rushing rivers. Nearly lost my salvage bicycle trying to wade through - had to lash it to a broken escalator railing with my last good bandages. Can hear the Iron Vanguard's traps snapping in the distance, their metallic *pings* echoing weirdly through the flooded tunnels. Probably catching more Fog Reapers than zombies at this point.

That new Mega-Mart location keeps appearing on my scavenging maps, but no way I'm braving that death trap until the storm passes. For now, I'm rationing the last protein bars and listening to my fungal counter map out the hive's movements. The way these tunnels shake when thunder hits... makes me wonder what's digging beneath us, and what happens when it reaches the surface.
