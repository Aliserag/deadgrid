# Survivor Log - Day 31

**Location:** Central Station (Forgotten Tunnels)  
**Weather:** Relentless Spring Rain (Severity: 2/5)  

The dripping echoes through these tunnels never stop. Not since the world ended. Not since the fungi started growing through the concrete like some kind of... living cancer.  

Last night I tried signaling again with the radio. That same SOS pattern keeps repeating from somewhere deep in the university district. I swear I heard something moving in response - not human, not zombie. Something... else. The fungal Geiger counter went crazy for about thirty seconds before settling back to its normal ominous hum.  

The rain makes everything worse down here. Water seeps through cracks in the ceiling, turning patches of fungal growth into pulsating, glowing marshes. My vest is permanently damp now, chafing against the developing fungal sensitivity rash on my shoulders.  

Found something strange near the Flooded Passage - what looks like a Fog Dweller's fungal harvesting kit abandoned in the water. Took the serrated knife (better than my current one) and left one of our sterile bandages as trade. The Sporebound don't take kindly to theft, even from their Fog Dweller cousins.  

The Iron Vanguard's traps are getting more sophisticated. Nearly lost a foot to some kind of pressure-sensitive fungal mine near Transit Hub. Their war with the Fog Reapers is turning these tunnels into a death maze.  

**New Discovery:** That mysterious chamber near the barricade? It's an old university library annex. The books are mostly pulp now, but some of the metal shelves might be salvageable. The upper floors are unstable as hell though - one wrong step and you're buried under three stories of academic rubble.  

**Weather Impact:**  
- Movement speed reduced by 20% in flooded areas  
- Fungal growths become hyperactive (increased spore clouds)  
- Radio signals get intermittent interference  
- The constant dripping masks approaching threats  

Tomorrow I need to:  
1. Scout the library properly (carefully)  
2. Boil rainwater using the pressure cooker  
3. Figure out who's sending that damn SOS  

The hive under Transit Hub is getting louder. I can almost make out... patterns in the digging now.
