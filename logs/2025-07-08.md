# Survivor Log - Day 22

**Location:** Central Station  
**Weather:** The cloudy ceiling presses down on the tunnels like a damp blanket. That eerie half-light makes every shadow twitch at the corner of my vision. The fungal patterns on the walls seem more pronounced today—their bioluminescent veins pulse in time with my heartbeat.  

Last night I worked by flickering emergency lights, repurposing medical supplies into makeshift bandages and fungal poultices. The pressure cooker gurgled as it sterilized rags, filling the station with the scent of boiled herbs and damp concrete.  

Then I saw *him* again—Silas Mercer, the Fog Dweller. Just a silhouette beyond the Glass Canopy, those fungal tattoos glowing faintly through the mist. He left another warning symbol near the Ventilation Shaft: three concentric circles carved into a mushroom cap. The Caravan Collective's markings nearby suggest they're interpreting these too.  

The Iron Vanguard's territorial scratches have multiplied near the Transit Hub. Found one of their spike traps baited with rotting meat—clearly meant for more than zombies. Had to disable it quietly. My Fog Walker instincts kept me from triggering their tripwires in the low light.  

**Notable Events:**  
- Repaired two flashlights using circuitry from abandoned train controls  
- Fungal Attunement helped detect a nest of tunnel spiders before stumbling into their webs  
- Radio picked up static bursts that might be Morse code (will investigate tomorrow)  
- Food reserves dwindling—must scavenge the Supply Cache despite Vanguard presence  

**Status:**  
The whispers are louder today. Not just the Hive—something *else* echoing through the flooded passages. Sometimes it sounds like a woman singing.
