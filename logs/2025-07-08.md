# Survivor Log - Day 23

**Location:** Central Station  
**Weather:** The tunnels feel heavier today, the damp air clinging to everything. That strange fungal scent seems stronger—like it's reacting to the humidity. I swear the glowing patches pulse brighter when I'm not looking directly at them.

Last night I worked on reinforcing my gear by the flickering emergency lights. The constant dripping sounds made me jumpy, but I managed to jury-rig some extra protection into my vest using scrap from an abandoned train seat. The fungal patterns on the walls kept shifting in my peripheral vision—I think this "Fungal Attunement" is getting stronger. Saw a cluster of spores arrange themselves into what looked like a warning right before I found a tripwire the Iron Vanguard left near the supply cache.

Speaking of which—that radio signal. It cuts through the static at random intervals, always the same broken message: *"Control room...boost signal...circuit boards...before..."* The Fog Dwellers' markings point toward the Transit Hub, but that's where I last saw Iron Vanguard activity. 

Today I met Silas Mercer near the Glass Canopy. The way the light hit his fungal arm made the growths look like they were breathing. He didn't speak at first, just stared with those unsettling gray eyes before whispering *"The hive knows your scent now"* and vanishing into a maintenance shaft. I don't know if that was a threat or a warning.

**Resources:**  
- Ate the last of the decent food (Morale -5)  
- Found 1 medical supply in an abandoned first aid kit  
- Pressure cooker condensed some drinkable water from the tunnel's humidity  

The Watchers left fresh markings near the generator room—their fungal documentation is getting more elaborate. Meanwhile, the Caravan Collective's symbols now appear near every flooded area, as if marking safe passages. 

Tomorrow I'll need to decide: risk the Transit Hub for those radio parts, or try to negotiate with Mercer's group. The way his fungal arm pulsed when he mentioned the hive...I think he's more connected to whatever's happening down here than he lets on.
