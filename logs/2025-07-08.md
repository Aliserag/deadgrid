# Survivor Log - Day 24

**Location:** Central Station  
**Weather:** The cloudy ceiling presses down on the tunnels today, turning every emergency light into a hazy halo. The diffused light makes the fungal growths seem to pulse faintly, like they're breathing in time with the dripping water. I swear some of them turned toward me when I walked past.

---

Last night's crafting session left my hands raw from twisting scrap metal into makeshift barricade reinforcements. The cloudy weather made it harder - without clear moonlight through the broken glass ceiling, I had to work by flickering emergency lights that made shadows dance like reaching hands. 

I was halfway through reinforcing my vest when I noticed it - the fungal stains on my tools were glowing brighter than usual. That's when the radio crackled to life with that distress signal. A woman's voice, choked with panic: *"Central Station... fungal core... they're not dead they're changing..."* Then static. The signal came from somewhere near the Supply Cache, where those strange bioluminescent trails I've started noticing seem to converge.

This morning I found fresh Fog Dweller markings near the Ventilation Shaft - fungal growths deliberately arranged in arrow patterns pointing toward the Mysterious Chamber. Mossback's people? A warning? Or bait? The Iron Vanguard's traps are getting more aggressive too - nearly lost a boot to a spring-loaded spike pit near Transit Hub.

My new... sensitivity to the fungal networks is growing stronger. Sometimes when I touch the walls, I get flashes of movement through the tunnels like the fungi are whispering secrets. It's how I found that hidden stash of medical supplies behind what looked like solid concrete. 

**Objectives Today:**
1. Follow the fungal trails toward the Supply Cache (distress signal origin)
2. Decide how to handle the Fog Dweller signs - ignore, follow, or destroy?
3. Check the Maintenance Depot for more crafting materials
4. Boil water while the cloudy weather keeps the zombies sluggish

The Watchers left fresh graffiti by the Generator Room: *"THEY WATCH THROUGH THE FUNGI"* Charming. Maybe today I'll find out who "they" are.

---
