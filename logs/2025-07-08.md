**Survivor Log - Day 28**  
*The Forgotten Tunnels - Maintenance Depot*  

The storm rages aboveground like some angry god, its fury muffled but still palpable through layers of concrete and steel. Water seeps through cracks in the ceiling, forming eerie stalactites that drip onto rusted train cars. My reinforced flashlight flickers in time with the emergency lights—both struggling against the damp darkness.  

I spent last night listening to the tunnels *breathe*. The fungal growths pulsed like they were digesting the storm, their bioluminescence flaring with each thunderclap. My skin itches where the spores landed yesterday. The Geiger counter chitters at random intervals, its readings spiking whenever I face the Collapsed Tunnel. Something’s moving in there. Not zombies. Something *wetter*.  

**Rest Results:**  
- The storm masked all noise, letting me sleep deeper than expected (+10% health recovery).  
- Dreamt of the Watchers’ fungal warnings—their symbols now glow faintly in my mind’s eye (*Fungal Instincts* skill progressed).

**Notable Changes:**

- A new Watcher message appears near the Generator Room: *"THEY BURROW WHERE IT WEEPS"* carved into a fungal mass.
