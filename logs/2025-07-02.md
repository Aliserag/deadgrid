# Survivor Log - Day 19

**Location:** Central Station  
**Weather:** Thick fog clinging to the tunnels like ghostly fingers  

The fungal growths seem to pulse in time with the flickering emergency lights today. I swear they've spread overnight, those bioluminescent veins creeping further along the walls. The fog makes everything worse - sounds bounce wrong down here, making every drip of water sound like footsteps. Twice now I've spun around expecting to see one of those tunnel runners behind me, only to find empty air.

Found something strange near the Glass Canopy this morning. A little package wrapped in blue plastic, tucked between two fungal stalks like it was waiting for me. Medical supplies inside - real treasure these days - and a note that just says "Follow." No signature, just that single word written in what looks like charcoal. The Watchers' work? Or something else moving in these tunnels?

The Iron Vanguard's markings have appeared overnight near the Transit Hub. Crude knife scratches showing their emblem - a gear with a skull in the center. They're getting bold. I can smell their fires sometimes when the air currents shift. 

That fog... it's playing tricks with the hive whispers. The voices sound closer today, like they're right behind the concrete walls. My reinforced flashlight barely cuts through the gloom, the beam diffusing after just a few meters. Found myself talking back to the echoes at one point before I caught myself. That's how it starts, isn't it? First you answer them, then you join them.
